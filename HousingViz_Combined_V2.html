Housing Market

Buying a home whether to live in it or for investment is stressful. You have to look at a lot of data from a lot of sources. We think we can make it easier for you.

We built some visualizations to make your search easier and fun!

Affordability

The graphs below show the average affordability index ( Price to Income Ratio ) and what your distance is with respect to the average in terms of affordability index ( Price to Income Ratio) Explore the graphs by choosing a year, a location and entering your annual income.

Investment

This chart shows average rent price ratio for different type of properties. On average, 2 bedroom and 3 bedroom properties have relatively high rent price ratio.

Two charts below gives a high-level overview of state level rent price ratio and the YOY ratio change.
